Knitting and Crochet

Whats the difference?

Knitting and crochet are both ways of making a fabric out of a strand of yarn. Knitting uses two needles to create rows of stitches that are held on one needle. Crochet uses a single hook to create individual stitches.

How to Crochet

Here's how to make a simple granny square.

Materials

To get started you will need:

  • crochet hook
  • yarn
  • darning needle
  • scissors

Stitches

  • ch = chain
  • sc = single crochet
  • dc = double crochet
  • slst = slip stitch

Method

  1. First make a magic loop, or ch3 and join to the first chain
  2. ch2, 2dc, ch3
